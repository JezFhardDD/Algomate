Algomate is an interactive educational application designed to help students understand and master Data Structures and Algorithms through hands-on simulation. Instead of only reading code, users can visualize how concepts like data structures, searching, and sorting algorithms work in real time, with guided explanations and step-by-step execution. Algomate combines lecture-style demonstrations with assessment-based simulations where learners actively perform operations, receive feedback, and earn scores based on accuracy and efficiency. By blending visualization, interaction, and performance tracking, Algomate transforms abstract algorithm concepts into engaging and practical learning experiences.
